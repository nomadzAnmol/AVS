Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) - globally recognized strategy for effective waste management through :-

  1. robust
  2. transparent
  3. accountable systems

In India, EPR compliance stands apart from financial and business compliance by prioritizing environmental sustainability.

Importance of EPR Consultants in India

EPR involves proactive measures, embracing circular economy principles, and requires collaboration with various stakeholders.
Given the frequent policy changes, expert guidance is crucial and so EPR consultant in India plays an essential role in effectively navigating this evolving landscape.

AVS: Comprehensive EPR Solutions for PIBOs & PWPs Compliance

AVS provide comprehensive one-stop-solution by engaging with Producers, Importers, and Brand Owners (PIBOs) - who are mandated to comply with regulations and Plastic Waste Processors (PWPs) - who generate credits , by offering end-to-end assistance.
We not only ensure compliance but also offer comprehensive retrofitting support for feeding mechanisms or material changes and so, by this approach we ensures that you achieve the maximum benefit from a single point of contact.
