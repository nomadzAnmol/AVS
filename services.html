Carbon Inventory Services

Our specialty is in calculating and analyzing carbon footprints for organisation, projects, products, and events. We are able toprovide a comprehensive overview of the impacts of greenhouse gases by covering Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions.
Here's a closer look at our key areas of focus:
Organisation Carbon Footprints: We calculate emissions from different business activities, such as Thermal applications, fleet operations, electricity consumption, and emissions from supply chains.
Product Carbon Footprints: We calculate emissions across a product's entire lifecycle, from cradle to gate, gate to grave or cradle to grave
Because Scope 3 emissions account for the majority of most firms' carbon footprint, our services are developed to assist these organizations in quantifying and managing these value chain emissions; this gives them the capability of working collaboratively with suppliers so that impacts up the chain could be minimized effectively.
Major Gains from Our Services:
- Insight into Supply Chain Emissions Hotspots: Identify and address, areas with the highest emissions in your supply chain.
- Environmental Accountability & Stakeholder Trust: Clearly demonstrate environmental accountability and cultivate stakeholder confidence.
- Integrates with International Reporting Standards: Ensures conformity of your organization with the global standards, for instance, the Carbon Protocol, in reporting.
By partnering with AVS, you gain access to expert guidance and customized solutions that transform your sustainability challenges into actionable and impactful results. Let us help you navigate the complexities of climate action with precision and confidence.

Carbon Financing

At AVS, we specialize in securing carbon finance for both large and small-scale Carbon Reduction/Removal projects. Our goal is to close the revenue gap by generating and trading carbon credits at the best possible prices. We work with all available carbon mechanisms and provide tailored advice based on the unique factors of your project.
Our Process:
1. Engage: Ensure your comfort at every step, guiding and supporting you through entire process.
2. Estimate: Our experts measure your carbon footprint and identify potential reductions.
3. Monitor: We establish precise progress tracking for your energy projects and provide complete documentation support.
4. Get Validated: We assist in getting your project validated by a reputed DOE.
5. Monetize: As a reward for contributing to a better world, we secure the best prices for your eligible carbon credits.
With a team boasting decades of experience, AVS is prepared to elevate your projects to recognized market leadership in carbon advisory and trading, hence transforming your sustainability challenges into profitable solutions.

Carbon Offset

Carbon Offsetting: Efficiently meet your carbon targets with AVS. We assist corporates and industries in achieving their sustainability and regulatory goals through both in-setting and offsetting strategies. In-setting: We guide businesses from identifying projects within their sphere of influence to the actual implementation and management of these projects.
Offsetting: We provide a myriad of project options for businesses to choose from based on their preferences. These projects include community-based initiatives such as cookstoves, biodigesters, sustainable carbon farming, community forestry, mangrove restoration, renewable projects and much more.
Our Process:

3. Selection: Select from meticulously mapped out project choices that align with your goals.
4. Trade: We assist you in trading the chosen project's carbon credits.

By partnering with AVS, you can navigate the complexities of carbon offsetting with confidence, ensuring that your sustainability efforts are impactful and recognized.

Net Zero

Achieving Net Zero is a comprehensive process that involves reducing greenhouse gas emissions as much as possible and offsetting the remainder. At AVS, we provide a structured approach to help organizations reach their Net Zero goals efficiently and effectively.
Our Process:
1. Engage: Evaluate your current carbon footprint and identify key areas for emission reductions.
2. Strategy Development: Create a customized Net Zero roadmap tailored to your organization's unique needs and goals.
3. Implementation: Execute the strategies through energy efficiency improvements, renewable energy adoption, and sustainable practices.
4. Monitoring: Continuously track progress and adjust strategies to ensure targets are being met.
5. Offset: Utilize high-quality carbon offset projects to neutralize any remaining emissions, ensuring compliance with Net Zero standards.
By partnering with AVS, you gain access to expert guidance and innovative solutions that make the journey to Net Zero achievable and impactful.
