Electrical safety

AVS Electrical Safety Audit Services
AVS provides essential electrical safety audits as a key component of comprehensive safety management. Our audits help organizations proactively manage electrical risks, ensuring a safe working environment while reducing maintenance costs and improving efficiency.
We identify potential electrical safety issues, ensure regulatory compliance, and recommend risk mitigation measures. Our team of experts conducts document reviews, visual inspections, testing, and compliance checks, providing detailed recommendations for corrective actions and best practices. Process risk and safety

AVS Process Safety and Risk Assessment Services
AVS offers specialized Process Safety Services to prevent and manage large-scale fires, explosions, and chemical accidents in facilities handling hazardous materials, such as refineries and industrial sites.
Our comprehensive Risk Assessment approach helps organizations make informed decisions to minimize adverse outcomes across various sectors, including industrial processes, finance, and healthcare. Our experts identify potential risks from natural events, technical failures, and human errors, estimating their likelihood and consequences using historical data, expert judgment, and probabilistic models.
We assess financial loss, health, safety impacts, and environmental damage, prioritizing risks and recommending mitigation measures. Our team ensures ongoing compliance and safety improvements through continuous monitoring and follow-up audits.

Environmental compliance

AVS Environmental Compliance Services
At AVS, we ensure project proponents fulfill their commitments made during the Environmental Clearance process. We meticulously prepare and submit compliance reports while providing timely reminders about environmental obligations, aiming to protect the environment and mitigate any adverse impacts.
Our expertise extends to handling Consent to Establish (CTE) and Consent to Operate (CTO) applications. We thoroughly understand the application formats and anticipate potential queries from State Pollution Control Boards (SPCBs). This insight allows us to swiftly address client concerns, facilitating smooth project initiation and compliance.
